5. Decision Making

When you identify a migrant smuggling or human trafficking case, your initial priority should be the welfare of the victims. Special attention must be given to the needs of women and children. They may be sick, in need of urgent medical attention or they may be experiencing mental or emotional trauma.

After you have arranged for the care of the victims, you should focus on gathering evidence to identify and prosecute the offenders.
