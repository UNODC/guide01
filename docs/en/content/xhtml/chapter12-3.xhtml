12. Searching a Crime Scene

12.3. Rural Search

  1. Draw a sketch plan of the area or mark the area on a map.
  2. Divide the search area into manageable parts using a grid system.
  3. Search each area systematically and safely, overlapping where necessary.
  4. Complete the search in one area before moving to the next area.
  5. If you find evidence, record it in its position and seize it.

For information on seizing and packaging evidence, refer to the section on Evidence Preservation in Section 14
