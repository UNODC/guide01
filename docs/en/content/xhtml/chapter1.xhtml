1. Sources of Information

The best source of information on migrant smuggling and human trafficking is often the victims themselves. They may be able to provide information including the number of smugglers, routes taken, vessels or vehicles used and the location of safe houses. You can gather extra information from a several sources. These can be divided into 5 categories:

 

Open Source:

  • TV/Radio
  • Print media
  • Internet

Workers:

  • Fishermen
  • Construction workers
  • Street vendors
  • Sex workers

Service Providers:

  • Taxi drivers
  • Bus operators
  • Social workers
  • Shipping agents
  • NGOs

Criminal Sources:

  • Informants
  • Suspects

Official Sources:

  • Officers from your agency
  • Other government agencies
  • International agencies
  • Neighboring Border Liaison Offices
